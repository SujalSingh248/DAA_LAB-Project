Image Compressor

Tiny, no-nonsense tool. Works in your browser. No uploads.

Drag & drop images here or click to choose files (JPG/PNG/WebP)

Tips

  • Set Max Width/Height to 0 if you don’t want resizing.
  • PNG with lots of transparency may compress better as WebP.
  • We keep everything on your device. Nothing gets uploaded.
